About the Game

Forest Rescue: Match 3 Puzzle is an exciting and colorful match-3 puzzle game where players team up with forest animals to protect their home from invading lumberjacks.

Match blocks of the same color, complete unique objectives, and advance through levels to save the forest from destruction. Along the way, unlock new characters and power-ups that will aid you in your mission.

How to Play

  • Match Blocks

    Match three or more blocks of the same color to clear them and earn points.

  • Complete Objectives

    Complete level-specific objectives by collecting items or removing obstacles.

  • Use Power-Ups

    Use special power-ups and animal abilities to clear challenging puzzles.

  • Plan Your Moves

    Strategize carefully, as limited moves require thoughtful decisions to complete levels.

  • Progress Through Levels

    Progress through hundreds of levels, each with increasing difficulty and new challenges.

  • Join Daily Events

    Participate in daily events and challenges to earn rewards and unlock bonus content.

Game Features

  • Adorable Animal Characters

  • Challenging Levels

  • Power-Ups and Abilities

  • Save the Forest

  • Special Events & Challenges
